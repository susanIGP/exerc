Switzerland

Switzerland, offically Swiss Confederation, Country, Central Europe, Area: 15,943 sq mi (41,291 sq km). Population: (2023 est.) 8,864,000. Capitals: Bern (administrative), Lausanne (judical). The population is German, French, and Italian. Languages: German, French, Italian, Romansh (all offical, with Romansh used locally). Religions: Christianity (Roman Catholic Protestant); also Islam. Currency: Swiss franc. Switzerland is divided into three regions: the meadow-covered Jura Mountains; the central Mittelland, a rich agricultural and ubranized area; and the lofty crags of the Alps.
